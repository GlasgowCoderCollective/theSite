About us

Amongst the bustling tech networking scene in Glasgow, lives GlasgowCoders.

We aim to fill the gap as a community for adult coders at all levels to code, learn, inspire, collaborate... The list is endless! We're an informal yet friendly, hands on bunch with weekly meets. Think laptops, drinks, and bad jokes
