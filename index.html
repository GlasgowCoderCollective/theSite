glasgowCoderCollective

Coffee, code, cake, chat

Amongst the bustling tech scene in Glasgow, lives the glasgowCoderCollective.

We aim to fill the gap as a fun, social community for adult coders at all levels of experience to code, chat, learn, inspire, collaborate... The list is endless! We want friendships to evolve amidst coffee, cake, chat and coding We're informal and friendly, with weekly meets on Thurdays evenings at GCU library 6.30pm+. Think laptops, drinks, and laughter! With the new year, we'll be starting monthly excursions to other tech events


So come along, beginners and experts alike, work on tutorials, personal projects and have fun!

If you want to become part of our community, join us!
